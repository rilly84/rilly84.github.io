A LONG TIME AGO, IN A GALEXY FAR FAR AWAY...

Turmoil has engulfed the Galactic Republic. The taxation of trade routes to outlying star systems is in dispute. Hoping to resolve the matter with a blockade of deadly battleships, the greedy Trade Federation has stopped all shipping to the small planet of Naboo. While the Congress of the Republic endlessly debates this alarming chain of events, the Supreme Chancellor has secretly dispatched two Jedi Knights, the guardians of peace and justice in the galaxy, to settle the conflict....

THE BATTLE OF ENDOR CONTINUES....

Han Solo and Princess Leia are trapped in the forest of Endor, awaiting the fate of the rebellion's attack on the deadly death star. Along with the help of the natives known as Ewoks, they work against driving the empire out of the galaxy...
